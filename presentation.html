Presented by: Saliya Wickramasingha
🔴 Problem Statement

Why Does UniStay Exist?

🚶

Manual House Hunting

Students walk through Pambahinna, Kumbalgama & nearby areas looking for vacant signs — stressful and time-consuming

💸

Pricing Transparency Issues

No centralized directory means owners can charge arbitrary prices with no benchmarks or student-side visibility

📍

Unknown Distance to Faculty

Students have no way to verify how far a property is from their specific faculty building before committing

🤝

No Direct Communication

Reliance on brokers and middlemen leads to exploitation, extra costs, and delayed information flow

🎯 Project Objectives

What UniStay Achieves

  • Develop a centralized web platform connecting students and boarding owners directly
  • Calculate precise distances from any boarding place to all 7 SUSL faculties using coordinate formulas
  • Provide advanced search and filter system (price, gender type, proximity to campus)
  • Implement secure role-based access separating student and owner experiences
  • Enable direct in-app messaging between students and owners — no middleman
  • Allow students to save and compare shortlisted properties via personalized favorites
  • Give owners an intuitive CRUD management panel with photo upload and coordinates

📐 Geospatial Calculation

Haversine Distance Formula

a = sin²(Δlat/2) + cos(lat₁) × cos(lat₂) × sin²(Δlng/2)
c = 2 × atan2(√a, √(1−a))
distance = R × c × 1.45
(R = 6371 km | ×1.45 = road walking factor)
📌

Step 1 — Extract Coords

Auto-extract lat/lng from Google Maps URL using regex patterns

📐

Step 2 — Calculate

Apply Haversine formula in JavaScript for each of 7 SUSL faculties

📊

Step 3 — Display

Instantly render computed distances (km) on listing cards and detail pages

  • Proven practical feasibility as a student welfare platform prototype for SUSL Belihuloya
